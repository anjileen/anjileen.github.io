About

Hi, my name's Anjileen and I have a passion for coding and design! I am a qualified web designer with a Diploma in Web Development and Design from Open Polytechnic. As well as a Diploma in Web and UX Design from Yoobee College of Creative Innovation.

Projects

Restaurant App

This is a prototype for a restaurant ordering system. It was designed to allow customers at resturants to order directly from their phone, rather than having to be served by a waiter. Coded with HTML/SCSS/JavaScript.

Accommodation Finder

The scenario for the project was to create an accommodation finder that allows those looking for short stays in NZ to find a room to stay. It has search filters to help users find the best match for them based on their preferences. Coded with HTML/CSS/JavaScript/JQuery.

Niche Market App

This was a group project between myself and my team member Richard. It is aimed at board game enthusiasts in NZ, and allows users to login, browse, create, edit and delete their listings. Richard coded the About and Manuals pages. All other pages were coded by me. Coded with MERN Stack - MongoDB/Express/React/NodeJS

Wordpress Theme

This project is a custom Wordpress theme built with HTML/CSS/JavaScript/PHP. It was created for a mock client who owns an interior design business, to showcase their projects and attract new business. It has functionality that allows the client to easily update all content themselves through the CMS, without the need for knowledge of coding.
